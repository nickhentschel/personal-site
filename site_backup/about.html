About This Site

I developed this site to be my final project for a course I took at Bentley University. I developed it using HTML5, and all pages are 100% standards-compliant. It also makes liberal use of CSS3 transitions, as evidenced by the hover effects on links, and the drop shadow on the logo. jQuery is used for the menu and the scroll-to-top arrow at the bottom of every page. The site is also built with a SilverStripe CMS backend, making administration and editing easy.
